Jay Young Park

About


Graphic Designer & Creative Director

Immigrating to the United States from South Korea in 1991, Park has been active in the sign, graphics, design & advertisement industry since the early '90s.

He has garnered over 30+ years of experience and partnered alongside a diverse range of clientele. Park is well-connected in the sign & graphics community and continues to maintain his small business, ADPOWER SIGN & GRAPHICS.

Park resides in Ridgefield Park, New Jersey with his wife and three children.
